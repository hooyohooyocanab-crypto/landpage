There is no setup charge for standard accounts. Enterprise onboarding may have fees.

Yes — we provide a free trial for new users so you can test features.

WhatsApp billing integrations and applicable taxes may be billed separately depending on region.

Our support team is available via email and chat. Priority support is offered on higher plans.

Some advanced modules are add-ons — they are optional and billed extra.

Yes — you can sign in from multiple devices depending on your plan limits.

Yes — inventory alerts and expiry notifications are core features.

09 Is Mediflux suitable for both retail and wholesale medical stores?

Yes — it is built to serve both retail and wholesale workflows.

Supplier & purchase order management is supported in the procurement module.

We use encryption and industry best-practices to protect customer data.
